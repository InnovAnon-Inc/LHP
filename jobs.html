Volunteer Positions

Anyone can do volunteer work on behalf of InnovAnon, Inc. If you practice an art or science as an act of worship, then it qualifies as a ministerial service which can be performed to appease InnovAnon.

Select any number of job titles and modifiers pertaining to the roles and responsibilities which you would like to undertake on behalf of InnovAnon, Inc.

Commitment Modifiers

Select your level of time-commitment.

  • Part-time: you may do some amount of secular work
  • Full-time: 24/7 ministerial services only

Experience Modifiers

Select your experience level.

  • Junior: you don't have any relevant experience
  • Associate: normal level of experience
  • Senior: a very high level of experience or reasonably skilled in managing a team

Primary Positions

These roles are central to our ministry. It's what we're all about.

  • Code-Monk: writes free code
  • IT-Monk: does free sys- and net-administration
  • Tutoring-Monk: free tutoring
  • Langar-Monk: cooks and serves free food
  • Gardener-Monk: plants free gardens
  • Applied Spiritual Scientist: exorcisms and stuff
  • Altar Attendant: leads/assists services/ministry
  • Music-Monk (Raagi): performs music
  • Treasurer-Monk: accepts and spends donations

Support Roles

These supportive roles help run the ministry and address our day-to-day living needs.

  • Driver-Monk: provides transportation and shipping services
  • Flier-Monk: combines Kinhin with propagating literature
  • Ecumenical Outreach Specialist: does seva for other congregations
