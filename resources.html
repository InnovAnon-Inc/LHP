    We can, e.g., provide transportation, help you acquire required items, or we can provide forbidden items for temporary use. *
  • Vow of Poverty:
    All our income is automatically forwarded (i.e., re-donated) to a temple that is not restricted by such vows.
    If a particular monk of your order needs to spend money, that temple can do so on his behalf (within the limits of the temple budget). *
