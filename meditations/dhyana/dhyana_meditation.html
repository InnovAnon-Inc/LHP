Dhyana Meditation

Focus on a particular object, usually a yantra

Procedure

  1. Fix your attention on a particular yantra.
  2. Close your eyes when you can visualize that yantra.

You may experience a loss of sense of self,
or you may perceive a lack of separation between yourself
and the visualized object.

Levels of Mastery

  1. Alpha Meditation
  2. Theta Meditation
  3. Delta Meditation
  4. Epsilon Meditation
