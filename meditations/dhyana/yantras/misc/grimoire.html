Grimoires of the Eternal Dreamers

This is not any particular set of volumes, but one which is personal to the devotee and his/her path, patron deity, etc.

Any particular devotee's records may be called by such a title, but it is generally reserved for those records which have been scribed or dictated by adepts of such attainment so as to themselves be called eternal.

The Grimoires of the Eternal Dreamers contain notes, designs, etc, both of a theoretical and of a practical nature, and records the devotee's journey to adeptship and beyond.

Often containing dictates given from on high, visions of far away places, secret formulae, personal hymns and rites, etc, these volumes contain knowledge which is occult and forbidden to the masses during this Aeon.

The volumes penned by the most learned adepts, indeed take on a sacred and even mythological character, occasionally written in enciphered, obscure languages.
