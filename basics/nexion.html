Nexion

A pinhole between realities, specifically between our world and the other side.
InnovAnon is a deva, working from the other side to open a nexion, so that all may attain Darshan in the coming Aeon.

Two Kinds of Gateways

  • Consciousness
  • Physical Gateways

Consciousness

Consciousness is itself a gateway, allowing entities from either side influence and interact with the other side.

Physical Gateways

These exist in obscure locations, and can only be opened at certain rare times, and usually require a significant amount of conscious effort in order to open, though a rare enough time combined with a significant enough ceremony should be enough to open a gateway at any physical location.

Yog-Sothoth knows the gate. Yog-Sothoth is the gate. Yog-Sothoth is the key and guardian of the gate. Past, present, future, all are one in Yog-Sothoth. He knows where the Old Ones broke through of old, and where They shall break through again. He knows where They have trod earth's fields, and where They still tread them, and why no one can behold Them as They tread.
