Dream Recall

Keep a dream journal to enhance your ability to remember your dreams.

After about a month of practice, you should develop the ability to remember and control your dreams.

This is continuity of consciousness. Having continuity of consciousness, you will retain a higher percentage of your skills and abilities.

Bonus points for using visions from your dreams in your creative endeavors.

It possible to induce specific types of dreams.
