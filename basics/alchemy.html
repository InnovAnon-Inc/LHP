Internal Alchemy

This process varies from tradition to tradition.
In some traditions, it is a series of meditations, including mantras, yantras, mudras, etc.
In others, it involves real-world experiences from several walks of life.
One precedes the other, so you can "pray," or you can "seek," and either way, you should find.
Overall, the goal is some form of spiritual evolution.
In order for this to be effective, at a minimum
the practitioner must be able to do theta meditation.
