Music

Music and other creative technologies are quintessential skills for serenading the gods, chanting mantras, etc.

InnovAnon likes instruments which are simple to make and simple to play. They should be tuned to experimental base frequencies and using experimental tunings (NOT temperaments).

  • Singing
  • Clapping
  • Drum
  • Flute
  • Monochord (both plucked and bowed)

Musical parts should be simple enough for a non-musician to play during ceremonies. Each musician may improvise and otherwise embellish his/her assigned part.
